# Privacy Policy for ShirtScanner

**Last updated:** Jul 6, 2026

ShirtScanner ("the App") is a photo-to-3D scanning tool that lets you turn photos of shirts and bodies into 3D models for viewing and editing. This policy explains what happens to your data when you use it.

## What we collect

The App does not require an account or login, and we do not collect your name, email address, or any other personal identifying information.

When you use the scanning features, the App collects:

- **Photos you choose to upload** — either a photo of a shirt or a photo of a body, which you provide by taking a picture or selecting one from your photo library.

We do not collect location data, contacts, analytics, or advertising identifiers, and the App does not include any third-party advertising or tracking SDKs.

## How your photos are used

1. When you upload a photo, it is sent from the App to our backend server.
2. Our server sends the photo to **Tripo3D**, a third-party 3D-generation service, which processes the image and generates a 3D model.
3. The resulting 3D model is sent back to the App and displayed to you.

We do not use your photos for any purpose other than generating the 3D model you requested. We do not sell, share, or use your photos for advertising, and we do not use them to train any AI models.

## Data storage and retention

- **On our server:** Generated models and uploaded images are stored temporarily on our backend so the App can retrieve them. This storage is not permanent and is regularly cleared, including whenever we update the backend software. We do not keep a long-term archive of your photos or models.
- **On your device:** If you choose to save a scan to your history within the App, a copy of the photo and the model's name/date are stored locally on your phone. This local history is not sent to us or to any third party — it stays on your device unless you delete it yourself within the App.
- **Third party (Tripo3D):** Photos sent to Tripo3D for processing are subject to Tripo3D's own privacy policy and data handling practices. We encourage you to review their policy if you'd like more detail on how they handle submitted images.

## Your choices

- You can delete any saved scan from your in-app history at any time (long-press the item and choose delete).
- Since there is no account system, there is no separate account to delete — uninstalling the App removes all locally stored data.

## Children's privacy

The App is not directed at children, and we do not knowingly collect information from children under 13 (or the relevant minimum age in your region).

## Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
